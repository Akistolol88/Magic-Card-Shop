Magical Theory at Tolaria

The enchantments developed during Urza's research represent breakthrough magical theories. From land acceleration to card selection, these spells showcase the innovative thinking that defined the Academy's approach to magic.

Resource Acceleration

Exploration broke new ground in resource management, allowing explosive early game development.

Information Advantage

Attunement and Abundance provided new ways to filter through your deck for the right cards.

Tutoring Effects

Enlightened Tutor became a cornerstone of artifact and enchantment-based strategies.

Hidden Surprises

Cards like Hidden Gibbons introduced unexpected elements to battlefield interaction.
