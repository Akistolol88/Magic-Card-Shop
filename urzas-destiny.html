About Urza's Destiny

Release Information

Release Date: June 7, 1999

Set Size: 143 cards

Set Symbol: Urza's face profile

Mechanics

Echo: Final iteration of the echo mechanic from the block.

Cycling: Continues to provide card selection and graveyard interactions.

Artifact Synergy: Strong focus on artifact-based strategies and combos.

Story

Urza completes his preparations for the Phyrexian invasion. He creates the Legacy artifacts and implements his final plan to defend Dominaria, setting the stage for the events of the Invasion block.

Competitive Legacy

Urza's Destiny completed one of the most powerful blocks in Magic history. Cards like Metalworker and Rofellos became cornerstones of artifact and ramp strategies respectively.

The Legacy of Urza's Block

The Urza block stands as one of the most influential and powerful in Magic's history. From the legendary lands of Urza's Saga to the game-changing artifacts of Urza's Destiny, this block fundamentally shaped competitive Magic and introduced mechanics and power levels that are still felt today.

Revolutionary Power Level

Many cards from this block are banned or restricted across multiple formats due to their incredible power.

Artifact Innovation

The block pushed artifact synergies to new heights, creating entire archetypes that persist in competitive play.

Story Impact

The culmination of Urza's story arc set the stage for the Phyrexian conflict that would define Magic's narrative for years to come.
