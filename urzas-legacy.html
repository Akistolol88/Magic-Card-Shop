Urza's Legacy (1999)

The second set in the Urza block continues Urza's experiments at the Tolarian Academy. Despite a catastrophic temporal disaster, Urza presses on with his research, creating powerful artifacts and recruiting allies for the coming war against Phyrexia.

About Urza's Legacy

Release Information

Release Date: February 15, 1999

Set Size: 143 cards

Set Symbol: Gear and crystal

Mechanics

Echo: Continued from Urza's Saga, requiring additional payments to keep permanents.

Cycling: More cycling cards that provide card selection and graveyard synergies.

Kicker: Alternative costs that provide additional effects when paid.

Story

A temporal disaster devastates Tolaria Academy, but Urza continues his research. He begins recruiting allies including the angel Radiant and exploring new planes in his search for weapons against Phyrexia.

Tournament Impact

Urza's Legacy provided many tournament staples, including the infamous Memory Jar which was quickly banned, and the beloved Rancor which remains a favorite in aggressive decks.
