Urza's Saga (1998)

The first set in the Urza block tells the tale of Urza's academy on Tolaria and his studies of artifacts and temporal magic. This set introduced some of the most powerful cards in Magic's history, including the legendary Tolarian Academy and other powerful lands.

About Urza's Saga

Release Information

Release Date: October 12, 1998

Set Size: 350 cards

Set Symbol: Tolarian Academy

Mechanics

Cycling: Pay a cost, discard this card: Draw a card.

Echo: Pay the echo cost during your next upkeep or sacrifice the permanent.

Free Spells: Spells you can cast without paying their mana cost under certain conditions.

Story

After the Brothers' War, Urza dedicates his life to preparing for the inevitable Phyrexian invasion. He establishes the Academy at Tolaria to study temporal magic and artifacts.

Legacy

Urza's Saga is considered one of the most powerful sets ever printed, with many cards banned or restricted in various formats due to their overwhelming power level.
