Urza's Mechanical Mastery

The artifacts of Urza's Saga represent the culmination of thousands of years of research and experimentation. Each piece demonstrates Urza's growing understanding of the multiverse and his preparation for the coming Phyrexian invasion.

Mana Acceleration

Powerstone technology allows for incredible mana production, enabling massive spells and combinations.

Control Elements

Artifacts like Smokestack and Null Rod provide powerful ways to control the battlefield.

Utility Tools

Devices like Voltaic Key unlock synergies between different mechanical components.

Time Magic

Temporal Aperture represents Urza's experiments with time manipulation at Tolaria.
